## Business Insights

### Customer Segmentation
High-value customers represent the largest segment and contribute the majority of the revenue. Medium and low-value segments represent smaller but important growth opportunities.

### Product Performance
A small number of products generate most of the revenue, indicating a strong Pareto distribution in product sales.

### Seasonal Revenue Trends
Revenue peaks in January, October, and November, suggesting strong seasonal demand likely tied to holidays and gifting periods.

### Customer Behavior
The business benefits from a strong loyal customer base with high repeat purchasing behavior.

---

## Strategic Recommendations

**1. Strengthen Customer Loyalty**
Introduce loyalty programs and personalized offers to retain high-value customers.

**2. Upsell Medium Value Customers**
Implement targeted promotions and bundle offers to increase average order value.

**3. Optimize Product Strategy**
Focus marketing and inventory on top-performing products to maximize revenue.

**4. Seasonal Campaign Planning**
Increase marketing efforts during peak months such as January and the holiday season.

**5. Boost Off-Season Sales**
Run promotional campaigns during low revenue months to maintain steady sales performance.


## Business Problem

Retail businesses often struggle to understand which customers drive the most value and which products generate the highest revenue. This project analyzes transactional data to identify high-value customer segments, top-performing products, and seasonal revenue trends to support data-driven marketing and inventory strategies.
